December 6-8, Cavaliada, Poznan - Poland

Hungarian horse whisperer at Cavaliada!

http://www.cavaliada-poznan.pl/pl/news/zaklinacz_koni_na_cavaliadzie/

We invite you to watch work of uncommon horse trainer Gyula Mészáros, member of Hungarian national team, who quit sport career to go on the different patch with horses.
Student of great horsemen such as Ray Hunt, Richard Winters, Eitan Beth-Halachmy and many more… Watching their work and being around them let his own approach rise to the surface which he believes is innately is in everyone. Then all we have to do is find it, free it and dare to use it! We need to find our true nature!
“ It is not often that you see such young and promising talent. It is as if Julius Meszaros is a horse himself” – Eitan Beth-Halachmy (Cowboy Dressage)

Come and meet Gyula. Each day at Cavaliada he will be working with young horse in the round pen (pavilion 6)
