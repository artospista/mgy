Know me

In this part you can get an itrospection about me and about those who shaped my walk of life related to horses. But first of all let me tell in a few lines what all this mean to me, that I framed couple of years ago. (- I think the best a statement is the one that has to be changed the least as time goes by! I feel that this statement is that kind of.-)

Horses deserve better!
The experience that I live through, the happiness, that results from a different, natural approach to horses can not be described to a simple earthborn. Either as a human being, or trainer or horseman. It simply has to be done and we find ourselves on earthly heaven!!!
More frequently I think of a book from my childhood. Thinking back perhaps – although it seemed distant and unimaginable but – I trusted that once I also could be able to communicate with animals, on their language. Well, I tell it. The book was Dr. Doolittle!:)
Only now I interpret the meaning of the word through the eyes of an English man: "Do little!" As if the adequate approach to horses even that time was there for me. Meaning: Get the most by doing the least possible!...
In all this the most touching is that horses around us changes!... Because we change! (But only if we listen to them carefully.) Because we changed and we stopped acting as predators around them that is why they do not need anymore to be in a constant readiness for flight!
The relationship is fragile, but if we constantly proove them that we are different, ...more than that we are of their, than the "miracle" can not only be sustained but can be increased and the pale is the sparkling sky!
As fantastic it is, it is as frightening, because as time passes we have bigger and more serious responsibility towards the creature we "uproot" from nature. If we succeded to prove to him that we are the leaders, from that point we have to give answer of comfort and safety to the questions of the glittering eyes looking to us full of questions.
Let’s observe them the better we can, what and how they do with each other, than learn it and do accordingly whenever we wish to enter their world!
... So: "Let’s do all, so that we can be the best to our horses! "..., because

" Horses deserve better! "
